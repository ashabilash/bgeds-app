COMPANY OVERVIEW

BGEDS is a Solar & Industrial engineering consultancy operating in India, since 2010. The company formed with experts from renewable & engineering industries to provide services from site feasibility to construction engineering and from rooftop to large scale utility projects. BGEDS customizes the design & drawings for every project to meet the customer requirements. Company build with strong core values and cultures. We deliver design and engineering for both Solar and Industrial engineering.
