BGEDS Racking Structure Overview
BGEDS has vast experiences in developing Preliminary and Detailed design and drawings for racking structure based on AISC 360-10 in line with ASCE 7-10/7-16 in lines. While dynamic instability is critical for PV racking, team expertise in deriving the Design based on Wind tunnel test from RWDI, CPP, etc., BGEDS develops the most appropriate design based on DC system requirement and calculation package is stamped with our licensed PE Partners across the Globe. The team delivered more than 5 GW PV racking design worldwide for different racking solutions such as ground-mount Single-axis 1P /2P, Fixed, Rooftop and Car Port, etc., we use In-house developed sheet and FEA platform to develop design and drawings such as RISA, SAP, Soil works and AutoCAD, etc., We also delivered project based on EN, AZ, SNI, IS, SBC and other international codes.
