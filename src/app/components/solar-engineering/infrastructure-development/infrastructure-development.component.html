BGEDS Infrastructure Development Overview
BGEDS has a dedicated team to develop detailed engineering design and drawings for Plant Infrastructures. The team of experts from different disciplines commits to deliver the best designs for the following:
  • Main Control room (Architectural plan & Elevation, Civil & Structural drawings, Structural design)
  • Inverter Room (Civil & Structural drawings, Structural design)
  • Internal Roads (Undulation study, Link road feasibility study, Internal Road Plan drawings and Specification Report)
  • Boundary & Fencing (Topographical & Contour study, Wind speed analysis, Plan and Elevation drawings)
  • Flood and Drainage study (Slope Map analysis, Ground Water study)
